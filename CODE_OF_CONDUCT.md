# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free collaboration environment.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Focus on ideas and evidence
- Assume good intent while discussing trade-offs

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks
- Doxxing, threats, or intimidation
- Repeated disruptive behavior

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation for violations.
